Machine Description and Purpose

Having children is life changing event and loving your children is different and deeper type of passion and affections that you will only express with them. Loving and caring for small fragile baby that depend completely on you create a special link between parents and children. Link some parents will keep it so tight that result in hurting their children instead of helping them though it is act of love but have devastating result.

Older parents or new parents. Having children your own or adopt one. Raising them alone or in a family it does not really matter. What is really matter that you shape and balance your relationship with them and not overwhelm them with love or over care. To understand the complication of this relationship this machine will give you insight about children from the prophet perspective.

The prophet is inspirational book contain a collection of poetic essays about love, live, children, marriage, freedom, joy, sorrow, passion, pain and much more topics related to life and human emotions by the Lebanese American writer, visual artist, poet, and philosopher Kahlil Gibran.

Machine Interactions:

Using the interactions will fulfill your experience where you will be able to customize the poem background choosing darker or lighter mode depending on your preferer from the top buttons. Also, you can customize font size or color the poem lines to enhancement your visual experience and your engagement with the poem. In addition, the interactions on the right will allow you to absorb the poem in more comprehensive way since they allow you to replace the pronoun, they with the word children or to capitalize the keyword such as children, like, not and Bows. You can also hide the header of the poem or scramble its verses to promote your experience and literature interaction with it.

Click on "Reset" every time you need to return to default page or to redo any customization or interactions you clicked.

Children

By Gibran Khalil Gibran

Poetic Essay from The Prophet Book

A woman who held a baby against her bosom said, 'Speak to us of Children.

And he said:

Your children are not your children.

They are the sons and daughters of Life's longing for itself.

They come through you but not from you,

And though they are with you, yet they belong not to you.

You may give them your love but not your thoughts.

For they have their own thoughts.

You may house their bodies but not their souls,

For their souls dwell in the house of tomorrow, which you cannot visit, not even in your dreams.

You may strive to be like them but seek not to make them like you.

The archer sees the mark upon the path of the infinite, and He bends you with His might that His arrows may go swift and far.

Let your bending in the archer's hand be for gladness;

For even as he loves the arrow that flies, so He loves also the bow that is stable.

For life goes not backward nor tarries with yesterday.

You are the bows from which your children as living arrows are sent forth.
